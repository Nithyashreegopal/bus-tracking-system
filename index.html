❓ FAQs
Everything you need to know
General
Tracking
Cities
What is TrackMyBus?▼
TrackMyBus is a real-time bus tracking system for small cities. See live positions, ETAs, crowd levels — all free.
Which cities are covered?▼
Salem, Erode, Tiruppur and Namakkal with 16 routes and 32 buses. More cities coming soon.
Is it free to use?▼
Yes, completely free for all commuters. No registration needed to track buses.
How often does position update?▼
Every second — like real GPS tracking. The map always shows the most current location.
How do I track a bus?▼
Click Track Buses Now. Select city and route. All buses appear as live markers on the map.
What does clicking a bus show?▼
Bus ID, City, Route, speed, ETA to next stop, distance, crowd level and moving/stopped status.
What are crowd levels?▼
Low (few passengers), Medium (moderately full) or High (very crowded).
How many routes in Salem?▼
5 routes: Railway Station→Airport, Bus Stand→Yercaud, Shevapet→Suramangalam, Five Roads→Kondalampatti, Omalur→Steel Plant.
How many routes in Erode?▼
4 routes: Erode Stand→Bhavani, Erode Jn→Gobichettipalayam, Perundurai→Erode, Kangeyam→Collectorate.
Routes in Tiruppur & Namakkal?▼
Tiruppur: 4 routes. Namakkal: 3 routes including Namakkal→Rasipuram, →Tiruchengode, →Mohanur.
